About Us

My name is Shenkoya Babajide Oluwasegun Joel of SBOJ Designs, I will be giving introduction into this site. Basically, this site is to demonstrate an HTML and CSS static web design without any backend functionality. Please note that all images, and icons on this website are gotten from external open-source sources and they are only intended for exhibition purposes only.

Imperial Leathers is an abstract design intendended to demonstrate a company that deals in all kinds of leather products for men and women. Our leather is made of animal skin and we ensured great deal of caution and restraint from collection to finishing of our leather products. We added classy extra designs to meet your aesthetic needs.
